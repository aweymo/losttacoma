About

This work is a partial representation of the Tacoma Public Library Northwest Room's Lost Tacoma collection,

constituting 116 of the total 560 blueprints across 26 locations.

Scene 1

The technical drawings include both proposed buildings which were never constructed and constructed buildings which are now demolished.

Scene 2

The scans of the blueprints were not performed by the Tacoma Public Library and some cropping and levels of the image files are not ideal.

Scene 3

The technical drawings have been reformatted and restored for legibility.

Scene 4

Restoration, arrangement and description was performed by Andrew Weymouth on the part of the Tacoma Public Library Northwest Room.
