Scandinavian American Bank Hotel


Status: Unbuilt

Created: December 29, 1920
Address: 1102-04 PACIFIC AVE, TACOMA


Two of 59 technical drawings created by Sutton & Whitney on December, 29, 1920. The Scandinavian American Bank Hotel was a partially constructed but never completed building intended to be on the lot that the Pacific Savings and Loan building would occupy two years after the creation of these drawings.
