Donnelly Site Hotel


Status: Unbuilt

Created: January 1, 1922
Address: 742-50 PACIFIC AVE, TACOMA


One of 24 technical drawings for a proposed rebuilding of the Donnelly Hotel by Dugan, Sutton and Whitney in 1922. The Hotel originally opened in 1897 and was demolished in 1925.
