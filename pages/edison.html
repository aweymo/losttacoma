Edison School Annex


Status: Demolished

May 28, 1910


Address: 3102 S 59TH ST, TACOMA


One of 14 technical drawings for the Edison School Annex, created by Frederick Henry Heath on March 28, 1910.
Renamed in 1912 to the Barlow Annex after South Tacoma community activist Orin Watts Barlow. The structure was demolished in 2011.
