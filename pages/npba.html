N.P.B.A. Hospital


Status: Demolished

Created: March 12, 1923
Address: 801 E WRIGHT AVE, TACOMA


Two of 18 technical drawings created by St. Paul, Minnesota's Lambert Bassindale firm for alterations to the Northern Pacific Beneficial Association Hospital on March 12, 1923. The building was constructed in 1904 and demolished in 1973.
