Henschien Everds & Crombie


Lost Tacoma Locations: Carsten Packing Company Abattoir


Henschien Everds & Crombie were a Chicago firm that specialized in designing meat packing companies that complied with USDA standards. The company worked primarily with east coast companies, including Oscar Meyer.
