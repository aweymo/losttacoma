Dupont School Building Addition


Status: Demolished

September 8, 1941



Address: 1 BARKSDALE AVE, DUPONT


One of 32 technical drawings created for the Dupont School Building Addition, created by William Mallis on September 8, 1941.
The addition was built in 1943 before the school was turned into the Laughbon High School, closed due to school boundary disputes. Finally, the structure was converted into a theater before being destroyed by fire in 1969.
